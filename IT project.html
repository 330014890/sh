It project: establish a web page of skin care products company

The design is divided into three parts. The first part introduces the company's background, advantages and future development direction, the second part introduces the company's personnel and personnel recruitment, and the third part is the skin care product purchase platform.

Design scheme

By collecting data, then analyzing the data and formulating the corresponding template, then making and testing the web page by using HTML and CSS language through codepen software, and finally publishing the web page on the Internet.

Values

So that you can understand the cosmetics industry of the company, have a good browsing experience when browsing the web, and facilitate customers' shopping.
